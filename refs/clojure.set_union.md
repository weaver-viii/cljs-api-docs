
Source code:

```clj
(defn union
  ([] #{})
  ([s1] s1)
  ([s1 s2]
     (if (< (count s1) (count s2))
       (reduce conj s2 s1)
       (reduce conj s1 s2)))
  ([s1 s2 & sets]
     (let [bubbled-sets (bubble-max-key count (conj sets s2 s1))]
       (reduce into (first bubbled-sets) (rest bubbled-sets)))))
```
