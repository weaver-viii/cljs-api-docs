 <samp>
(__fnext__ coll)<br>
</samp>

---

Same as `(first (next coll))`

---

Example:

```clj
(fnext [1 2 3])
;;=> 2

(fnext [1 2])
;;=> 2

(fnext [1])
;;=> nil

(fnext [])
;;=> nil
```
