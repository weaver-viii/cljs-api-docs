Source code:

```clj
(defn distinct
  [coll]
  (let [step (fn step [xs seen]
               (lazy-seq
                ((fn [[f :as xs] seen]
                   (when-let [s (seq xs)]
                     (if (contains? seen f)
                       (recur (rest s) seen)
                       (cons f (step (rest s) (conj seen f))))))
                 xs seen)))]
    (step coll #{})))
```
