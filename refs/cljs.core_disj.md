Source code:

```clj
(defn disj
  ([coll] coll)
  ([coll k]
    (when-not (nil? coll)
      (-disjoin coll k)))
  ([coll k & ks]
    (when-not (nil? coll)
      (let [ret (disj coll k)]
        (if ks
          (recur ret (first ks) (next ks))
          ret)))))
```
