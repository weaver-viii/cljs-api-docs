 <samp>
(__nfirst__ coll)<br>
</samp>

---

Same as `(next (first coll))`.

---

Example:

```clj
(nfirst [[1 2 3] [4 5]])
;;=> (2 3)

(nfirst [[1 2] [3 4]])
;;=> (2)

(nfirst [[1] [2 3]])
;;=> nil

(nfirst [[] [1 2]])
;;=> nil
```
