---

If no `y`s are supplied, returns the negation of `x`, else subtracts the `y`s
from `x` and returns the result.

---

Example:

```clj
(- 1)
;;=> -1

(- 6 3)
;;=> 3

(- 10 3 2)
;;=> 5
```
