---

Returns the sum of nums.

`(+)` returns 0.

---

Example:

```clj
(+)
;;=> 0

(+ 1)
;;=> 1

(+ -10)
;;=> -10

(+ 1 2)
;;=> 3

(+ 1 2 3)
;;=> 6
```
