---

If no denominators are supplied, returns 1/numerator, else returns numerator
divided by all of the denominators.

---

Example:

```clj
(/ 6 3)
;;=> 2

(/ 6 3 2)
;;=> 1

(/ 10)
;;=> 0.1

(/ 1 3)
;;=> 0.3333333333333333
```
