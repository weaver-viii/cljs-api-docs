 <samp>
(__dotimes__ \[name n\] & body)<br>
</samp>

---

Repeatedly executes `body` (presumably for side-effects) with `name` bound to
integers from 0 through `n`-1.

Source code:

```clj
(defmacro dotimes
  [bindings & body]
  (let [i (first bindings)
        n (second bindings)]
    `(let [n# ~n]
       (loop [~i 0]
         (when (< ~i n#)
           ~@body
           (recur (inc ~i)))))))
```
